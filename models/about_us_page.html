The Story Behind El-Olam Special Home

El-Olam Special Home & Rehabilitation Center was founded to bring hope, love, and structured support to children living with disabilities. We believe that every child, regardless of their challenges, deserves equal opportunities to learn, grow, and live a joyful life.

Vision & Mission

  • To create a safe and nurturing environment for children with disabilities.
  • To empower children through rehabilitation, special education, and therapy.
  • To support families in understanding and caring for their children.

Target Beneficiaries

We provide support to children with conditions such as:

  • Cerebral palsy
  • Autism spectrum disorders
  • Down syndrome
  • Learning disabilities
  • Speech & communication disorders
